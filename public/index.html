Why Three Fat Arrows?

I wanted to create something that was funny, unique, memorable, tied back to the Bible, and represented our children.

Three
Represents the number of children that my wife and I have here on Earth. Read About Emma
Fat
Refers to the size and overall impact of the arrows.
Arrows
Refers to Psalm 127:3-5, in that our children are like arrows in the hand of a warrior. By God’s grace, to make a strong, positive impact on our world for Jesus.

In short, the name pays homage to the gifts God has given us in our children and the impact they will have.
